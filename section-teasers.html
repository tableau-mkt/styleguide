Teasers

Breif teasers of content used individually or in lists to link to the full view of the content.

Large Teaser

A large teaser with a high-resolution preview image, meant to be displayed as a single full-width item. An example use case would be to display each slide of a slideshow as a large teaser.

Example

Mapping Houston’s electricity usage.

Frank Bracco of the City of Houston Blog, visualizes the changing energy usage climate in Houston, Texas.

Download This dataset in Tableau

Markup

<div class="large-teaser">
    <div class="large-teaser__thumbnail thumbnail">
      <a href="">
        <img src="http://www.placecage.com/850/475"/>
      </a>
    </div>

  <div class="large-teaser__copy">
      <div class="large-teaser__title">
        <h4>
          <a href="">Mapping Houston’s electricity usage.</a>
        </h4>
      </div>

    <div class="large-teaser__description">
        <p>Frank Bracco of the City of Houston Blog, visualizes the changing energy usage climate in Houston, Texas.</p>

        <p class="large-teaser__link">
          <a href="#" class="link ">Download</a>
            <span class="text--label"> This dataset in Tableau</span>
        </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
