Typography

Font Families

All text is set in either Benton Sans or Merriweather. This includes body copy as well as page and section headers.

BentonSans is primarily used for section headers and titling in its Thin case, but it is also used in its Book case in buttons and other scenarios. Occasionally BentonSans Medium is used in article templates.

Merriweather is used in its Light case mainly for body copy and in the Stories and Articles sections.
