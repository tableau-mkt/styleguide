Content Promotions

Components meant to promote related or important content elsewhere on the site. Similar to a Teaser component, but more prominent and not meant to be used for long lists of content.

News Banner

A News Banner displays content to promote an important announcement to the user. A News Banner is displayed the first time a user visits the page and then stores a cookie as soon as they dismiss the message to not show the same message to the user again. Individual messages are identiified by their id attribute so it's important to make sure different messages have a unique ID.

Due to the nature of fixed positioned elements, the examples below don't function properly. This component is meant to be placed at the very top of a page in order to be displayed properly.

Meet Tableau 10

News Ticker

A slim auto-scrolling slider of note-worthy news items with a link.

Example

Tableau 10.2: Spatial files, Python and more. See what’s new in Tableau.
Gartner MQ: Five years a leader. Read the report.
News Label: Some description text of the news. Call to action link.

<div class="news-ticker">
    <div class="news-ticker__item">
      <strong>Tableau 10.2:</strong> Spatial files, Python and more. <a href="#">See what’s new in Tableau.</a>
