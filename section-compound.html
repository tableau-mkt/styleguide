Compound

Multi-facetted, styled (non-interactive) content display widget.

Page Hero

Heroes on need to accomplish two main tasks. First, they need to convey a specific story as simply and beautifully as they can in order to drive traffic. Secondly, they need to set the tone for the page they are setting up.

Very simple type and imagery treatments can create different feels for pages that need to stand on their own.

Example

Sub Title

Big Headline String

Call to Action

Markup

<div class="hero [modifier class]">

    <div class="hero__background">
      <div class="hero__background__inner" style="background-image: url(http://www.placecage.com/1366/540);">
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="hero__content">
      <div class="hero__content__inner content-container">
          <h4 class=" ">
            
              Sub Title
            
          </h4>
        
          <div class="hero__content__headline clearfix">
            <h1 class=" ">
              
                Big Headline String
              
            </h1>
          </div>
        
        
          <div class="hero__content__cta clearfix">
            <a class="cta cta--play " href="http://www/tableau.com">
              Call to Action
            </a>
          </div>

      </div>
    </div>
</div>

Messages

Display special messages to the user. Recommend putting multiple messages in <li> tags.

.messages--error .messages--warning

Example

  • My baby's comin home on July 14th!
  • Put down the bunny.

Markup

<div class="messages [modifier class]">
  <ul><li>My baby's comin home on July 14th!</li><li>Put down the bunny.</li></ul>
</div>

Quote

Stylized quoted text such as a pull quote or testimonial.

Example

Tableau is the best thing that ever happened to us. We've spent 90 percent of our time on the data and 10 percent on the tool. We had a significant ROI.

Cindy Sedlacek, Professor

Markup

<div class="quote [modifier class]">
  <p class="quote__text">Tableau is the best thing that ever happened to us. We&#x27;ve spent 90 percent of our time on the data and 10 percent on the tool. We had a significant ROI.</p>
  <p class="quote__byline">
    <span class="quote__byline__name">Cindy Sedlacek,</span>
    <span class="quote__byline__title">Professor</span>
  </p>
</div>
