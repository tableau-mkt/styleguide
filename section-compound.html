Basic Hero

A simple hero to be displayed at the top of the page containing the page title and any other information about the page content such as an eyebrow or a subtitle.

Example

An Eyebrow

Here is a Page Title

This is a bit more lengthy subtitle that explains a bit about the contnet below.

Extra content can go here such as a search bar or longer description

Markup

<section class="hero " >
  <div class="content-container">
      <p class="hero__eyebrow">
        An Eyebrow
      </p>
      <h1 class="hero__title">
        Here is a Page Title
      </h1>
      <h5 class="hero__subtitle">
        This is a bit more lengthy subtitle that explains a bit about the contnet below.
      </h5>
      <div class="hero__extra-content">
        <p class='text--centered'>Extra content can go here such as a search bar or longer description</p>
      </div>
  </div>
</section>
