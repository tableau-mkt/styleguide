Compound

Multi-facetted, styled (non-interactive) content display widget.

Quote

Stylized quoted text such as a pull quote or testimonial.

Example

Tableau is the best thing that ever happened to us. We've spent 90 percent of our time on the data and 10 percent on the tool. We had a significant ROI.

Cindy Sedlacek, Professor

Markup

<div class="quote ">
  <p class="quote__text">Tableau is the best thing that ever happened to us. We've spent 90 percent of our time on the data and 10 percent on the tool. We had a significant ROI.</p>
  <p class="quote__byline">
      <span class="quote__byline-name">Cindy Sedlacek,</span>
      <span class="quote__byline-detail">Professor</span>
  </p>
</div>

Basic Hero

A simple hero to be displayed at the top of the page containing the page title and any other information about the page content such as an eyebrow or a subtitle.

Example

An Eyebrow

Here is a Page Title

This is a bit more lengthy subtitle that explains a bit about the contnet below.

Extra content can go here such as a search bar or longer description

Markup

<section class="hero " >
  <div class="content-container">
      <p class="hero__eyebrow">
        An Eyebrow
      </p>
      <h1 class="hero__title">
        Here is a Page Title
      </h1>
      <h5 class="hero__subtitle">
        This is a bit more lengthy subtitle that explains a bit about the contnet below.
      </h5>
      <div class="hero__extra-content">
        <p class='text--centered'>Extra content can go here such as a search bar or longer description</p>
      </div>
  </div>
</section>

Messages

Display special messages to the user. Recommend putting multiple messages in <li> tags.

Examples

Notice message (Default)

.messages--notice

  • My baby's comin home on July 14th!
  • Put down the bunny.

Error message

.messages--error

  • My baby's comin home on July 14th!
  • Put down the bunny.

Warning Message

.messages--warning

  • My baby's comin home on July 14th!
  • Put down the bunny.

Markup

<div class="messages [modifier class]">
  <ul><li>My baby's comin home on July 14th!</li><li>Put down the bunny.</li></ul>
</div>
